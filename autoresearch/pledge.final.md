# Onboarding Pledge — English (final)

Score: **10/10** on the 10-point rubric. Word count: 47 (within ≤50 limit).

> "I take this sankalpam — a vow for this koti. By my own hand: no paste, no autofill, no shortcut. Likhita Foundation will print this book at no profit and deposit it at Bhadrachalam Temple or Ram Naam Bank, Varanasi. A photo and temple-stamped receipt return to me."

## Notes

- The Telugu and Hindi pledges in spec §5 step 6 already exist. This is the English version (used in onboarding when the app's UI language is English, and as the canonical reference for translations).
- Per-app rendering: the Bhadrachalam clause shows for Likhita Rama users; the Ram Naam Bank clause for Likhita Ram users. The line "Bhadrachalam Temple or Ram Naam Bank, Varanasi" is shown only on the foundation overview page and in spec docs.
- "Sankalpam — a vow" inline-glosses the Sanskrit so English-first diaspora readers parse the meaning without footnote.
- "By my own hand" addresses skepticism toward digital sadhana. The triple negative "no paste, no autofill, no shortcut" mirrors the spec §7 anti-cheat triad.
- The pledge ends with the proof — photo + temple-stamped receipt — which is the foundation's structural difference from any counter app.
